ğŸ“ ALGORITHM

  Uses FARNEBACK OPTICAL FLOW to detect motion between frames.

  Frames with similar motion patterns are likely consecutive in time.

  The algorithm:
    1. Starts with any frame
    2. Finds the most similar frame (by motion)
    3. Adds it to the sequence
    4. Repeats until all frames are ordered
